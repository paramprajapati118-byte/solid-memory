About Steve Jobs

Steven Paul Jobs (1955–2011) was an American entrepreneur, industrial designer, and investor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. Jobs was widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution.

Personal Details

  • Full Name: Steven Paul Jobs
  • Born: February 24, 1955
  • Died: October 5, 2011
  • Nationality: American
  • Known For: Apple Inc., Pixar, NeXT

Major Achievements

Apple Inc.

Co-founded Apple and led the creation of revolutionary products like the iPhone, iPad, and MacBook.

Pixar Animation Studios

Turned Pixar into a world-leading animation studio, producing movies like Toy Story.

Product Design

Popularized minimalist, user-friendly design that changed consumer technology forever.
